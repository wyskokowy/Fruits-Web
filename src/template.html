Why fruits?

Five reasons for eating Five a day

Fruit are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium.

They're an excellent source of dietary fibre, which can help to prevent digestion problems.

They can help to reduce your risk of heart disease, stroke and some types of cancer.

Fruit are also usually low in fat and calories.

Fruit taste delicious and there's so much variety to choose from.
